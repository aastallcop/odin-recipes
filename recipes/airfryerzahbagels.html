Description

Everyone loves pizza bagels! Follow this easy and quick recipe to bring you back to the nostalgic days of a convenient frozen snack that we all love!

Ingredients

  • 2 plain bagels, split
  • 1 cup marinara/pasta sauce
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • freshly grated Parmesan

Pepperoni pizza toppings

  • Pepperoni slices
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese (4 oz)
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning to taste
  • 1/4 cup of veggies of choice: slices bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, olives

Margherita pizza toppings

  • 4 oz fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced (1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese is also fine)
  • Fresh basil leaves

Steps

  1. Preheat air fryer, following manufacturer's setting,to 400 degrees F
  2. Cut your bagels in half.
  3. Brush olive oil on your bagels, then spread an even layer of marinara sauce.
  4. Arrange your cheeses and toppings on top of the marinara sauce.
  5. Use a spatula to place your bagels into the air fryer basket.
  6. Cook for 5-7 minutes at 400 degrees or until the edges of the bagel are crispy and cheese is melted and golden brown.
  7. Remove bagels from air fryer with a spatula. Finish with Parmesan cheese and add more basil leaves, if desired. Enjoy!
