Air fryer Chicken Taquitos

  • Return Home

Description

Everyone loves a good taquito! It reminds us of our summer days of walking down to a nearby 7/11 to grab a quick and delicious snack. Follow this quick and easy snack when you're on a time crunch!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shredded rotisserie chicken
  • 1 1/2 cups Mexican-blend shredded cheese
  • 3/4 cup sour cream, extra for serving
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground pepper
  • Hot sauce (optional)
  • 12 6-in corn tortillas
  • Cooking spray
  • Salsa and guacamole, for serving

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix the chicken, cheese, sour cream, cumin, chili powder, and a big pinch of salt and pepper. Season with hot sauce, if you desire it be extra spicy.
  2. Lay out 6 tortillas and divide half the chicken mixture. Pile the chicken mixture on one end of the tortilla and roll it up to make a taquito.
  3. Arrange taquitos seam-side down in air fryer basket. Spray the tops of the taquitos with cooking spray.
  4. Cook for 6 mins at 400 degrees F. Flip and spray the other side, and cook for another 2-4 minutes or until the taquitos are browned and crispy.
  5. Roll up the next batch while you wait for the first batch of taquitos to finish cooking. Repeat the cooking process for the last batch.
  6. Serve the taquitos with salsa, guac, and sour cream.
